I build memorable digital experiences

I am a Munich based designer from London specialising in Web/UI Design, UX, Graphic Design, Branding, Illustration. I currently work for Gini GmbH.

I love to make beautiful things that make peoples’ lifes better. Whether it be an app, that through real purpose and ease of use makes life easier. Or simply creating something that puts a smile on some ones face - I truly believe that good design makes the world a better place.

After studying psychology at university I began working in marketing roles for various London based multinational companies. Here I discovered my love for Photoshop, and became instantly obsessed. This led to leaving marketing behind to teach myself the principles of design as well as how to implement them, with Illustrator (my favourite tool), Photoshop and all the rest.

I founded Pixel and Ink LLP after re-locating to Berlin. Offering digital and print solutions, I worked with some great clients (including the Guardian Edinburgh International Television Festival, amongst others) on some awesome projects. After three years I decided that I really missed working in teams and was after a new challenge.

Shortly after moving to Munich I found the perfect opportunity at Gini. Here I work primarily with iOS apps, but also web apps, website design and development as well as all marketing and sales materials. In effect, all visual materials that come out of the company.

So that’s a (very) brief summary about me, if you would like to get to know me better, or just have a chat about anything from design to comics to QPR (my beloved football team) please give me a shout!
