I build memorable digital experiences

I am a Munich based designer from London specialising in Web/UI Design, UX, Graphic Design, Branding, Illustration. I currently work for Gini GmbH.

I love to make beautiful things that make peoples’ lifes better. Whether it be an app, that through real purpose and ease of use makes life easier. Or simply creating something that puts a smile on some ones face - I truly believe that good design makes the world a better place.
